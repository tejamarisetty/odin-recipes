Chicken Tenders

These chicken tenders are crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken tenders
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. In another bowl, beat the eggs.
  3. In a third bowl, place the breadcrumbs.
  4. Dip each chicken tender in the flour mixture, then the egg, and finally coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the chicken tenders until golden brown and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
